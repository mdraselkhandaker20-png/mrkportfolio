About Me

Who I Am

Degree MSc — Engineering Business Management (Specialization: AI & Intelligent Systems)
University Multimedia University, Malaysia
RESEARCHER AI & IoT Researcher
WRITER Published Poet

I have a strong academic background in Science and Engineering, having completed my MSc in Engineering Business Management from Multimedia University, Malaysia, following a BSc from Sonargaon University, Bangladesh. My research interests focus on Artificial Intelligence, IoT-based intelligent systems, and machine learning applications in industrial quality monitoring, and my research has been submitted to ICTIM 2026 Conference and IEEE 2026 Conference, with a journal publication currently in progress.

I have practical experience in Python programming and data-driven system development, including Flask, HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript, as well as using GitHub for version control. These skills allow me to build intelligent and data-driven systems.

Beyond my academic work, I am also a published poet. My book "Meherban" reflects a deeply personal exploration of emotion, identity, and the human experience, highlighting my creative perspective alongside my technical background.
